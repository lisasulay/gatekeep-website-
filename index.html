Trust & safety

Gatekept knowledge — without the gatekeeping risk.

Real talk requires real safeguards. Here's how we keep it honest without putting anyone in danger.

Verification before it's posted

Recommendations go through a check before they're visible — no drive-by spam, no fake reviews.

Anonymous, honest posting

People share what they'd never say under their real name — without fear of being gatekept themselves.

No direct liability

Sensitive categories (babysitters, contractors) link only to already-verified companies — accountability sits with them, not GateKeep.

Sponsored, but earned

Businesses can pay to be listed — but underdeliver on real experiences, and they're dropped. Money doesn't buy trust here.

Aggregated, not siloed

We pull signal from multiple sources instead of trusting one platform's review pool.

Not a diagnosis engine

Health-related answers reflect real people's real experiences — framed as "here's what this commonly turns out to be," never a diagnosis.

How GateKeep makes money

Revenue that doesn't compromise trust.

GateKeep never sells ranking. Every revenue stream is designed so the businesses and users who behave well are the ones who win.

  • 1

    Verified sponsored listings

    Local businesses pay a flat monthly fee for a "Verified on GateKeep" badge and featured placement — pulled instantly if real-user feedback turns sour.

  • 2

    GateKeep+ premium subscription

    A user-facing subscription unlocking faster answers, the appointment finder, and the skincare/product breakdown tool.

  • 3

    Referral & booking fees

    A small commission when a user books a doctor, leases a car, or hires a verified service directly through GateKeep.

  • 4

    Anonymized trend insights

    Aggregated, privacy-safe local demand data (e.g. "most-asked-about services this month") licensed to city partners and local orgs.
